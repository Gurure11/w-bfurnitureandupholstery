About Elvis Wheels

Elvis Wheels, based in Vanderbijlpark, has been offering **excellent and reliable tyre and rim services for over ten years**. With two well-established branches, we specialize in:

  • Wheel balancing and alignment
  • Professional aluminium welding for cracked or broken wheels
  • Comprehensive suspension work, brake pad replacement, and shock replacement
  • Supply of both new and **affordable used tyres** (from R499 for used, R799 for new)
  • Expert rim repairs, meticulous rim polishing, and precise PCD hole changes
  • **Precision diamond cutting** to give your rims a unique, stunning finish

Our **commitment to quality, safety, and customer satisfaction** sets us apart in the industry. Drive in today and experience the Elvis Wheels difference!
